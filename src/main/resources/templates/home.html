About us

SaveWave is a modern budget tracking app designed to help individuals stay in control of their finances.

With powerful features like interactive charts and collaborative tools, your budget has never been easier.

Features

Expense & Income Tracking

Easily track your income and expenses in real-time with our intuitive interface.

  • Income reports
  • Expense categories
  • Daily summaries
Charts & Analytics

Interactive graphs and charts to help you visualize your financial health over time.

  • Pie charts
  • Line graphs
  • Monthly trends
User Accounts & Secure Access

Share your budget with family or partners and manage joint accounts easily.

  • Multiple users
  • Shared goals
  • Notifications
